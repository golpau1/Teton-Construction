A boutique collection of nine thoughtfully designed townhouses, nestled in the quaint and historic neighbourhood of Burleigh Heads.

Blending seamlessly with the area's rich heritage, The Violet introduces a modern twist — a contemporary lifestyle precinct that captures the essence of its natural surroundings. Each townhouse is crafted to reflect the balance of convenience and vibrancy, tranquility and serenity, offering residents a harmonious coastal way of life.

Set within lush green surrounds, The Violet is a celebration of modern living in a rejuvenated and evolving pocket of Burleigh Heads where heritage meets innovation.
